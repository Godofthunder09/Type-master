About TypeMaster

built for professionals, by a professional

Type faster. Work smarter.

TypeMaster is a free, no-login typing platform built for office workers and professionals. Better typing leads to better productivity, less fatigue, and more focus on what actually matters.

⌨️
Real Typing Tests
Time, word and Pro Mix modes with live WPM, accuracy and a real-time WPM graph.
📖
Structured Lessons
8 lessons from home row to professional email phrases.
🎮
Fun Games
Word Rain, Speed Rush and Precision Strike to build speed through play.
🔥
Keyboard Heatmap
Visual map showing exactly which keys you make the most errors on.
💀
Extreme Modes
Blind Mode, Sudden Death, Reaction Mode and Rhythm Mode for serious practice.
🔒
100% Private
No login, no signup. All data stays in your browser forever.
